San Francisco

2.7.2019

San Francisco, CA

The day after school ended, we immediately took off to enjoy our summer vacation. We drove down from Vancouver to enjoy the scenery and to take a more scenic route when coming back. San Francisco was as picturesque as you see in photos. We toured all the major tourist destinations, rented some bikes to enjoy the beaches and parks, and drove north to do some hiking. Coincidentally, the famous Pride Parade went right down the street our hotel was on. It was the best parade I have ever seen, with lively DJs and gigantic floats. Some good restaurants to eat at is The Grove for amazing food with a unique ambiance and Tony's Pizza Napoletana for the best pizza in the city. SF MOMA is worth a stop for amazing art and you should try the trams at least once, however touristy they might seem.
