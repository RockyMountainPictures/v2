Whale Watching

22.5.2019

Strait of Georgia, BC

Each year some of our old neighbors from Ottawa come out west to visit Vancouver, we always get them out on the sea to go whale watching. This time was no exception. The best part about it was that they were available on the weekend, which meant I could go whale watching for the first time! It also was the day I bought my X-T20, but it did not come with me whale watching since the XTi had better zoom capabilities. It was the best day to go whale watching. It was sunny, low winds and nobody threw up! We even got to see killer whales on our way back from exploring around the coast of Vancouver Island!
