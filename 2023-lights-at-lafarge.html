Lights at Lafarge (2023)

28.12.2023

Coquitlam, BC

Every year, the city decorates Lafarge Lake in over a hundred thousand lights for the winter holidays. I've been plenty of times, and each year they have always manage to add something new and refreshing. This year was no exception, as they created an Alice in Wonderland themed section. I focused on said section, along with experimenting with timelapses of the people walking around the lake.
