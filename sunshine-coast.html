Sunshine Coast

20.11.2018

Gibsons, BC

We had a jam-packed day trip to the Sunshine Coast. Woke up bright and early, took off to the ferry and arrived at Gibsons before our breakfast place we chose in advance even opened. So we went down to the pier and enjoyed the peaceful morning taking pictures and walking Parker. After our breakfast at Drift Cafe & Bistro, we started driving north to check out some sculptors that my dad was interested in. We bought some Christmas gifts from them and continued along up to Earls Cove, where we walked the dogs along the ferry pier. On our way back to Gibsons, we stopped at Halfmoon Bay to let Parker run around on the off-leash beach. We made it back to Gibsons to catch the 5:25pm sailing. It was a big day, but it was fun.
