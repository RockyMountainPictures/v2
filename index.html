A little about this website

My name is Max and welcome to Rocky Mountain Pictures, my photography portfolio. 

This is the second iteration of this project. A complete redesign and reimagination of my old site that I started as a passion project in grade eight. Since then, I have grown as a web designer and photographer, and have used those skills to present a portfolio to showcase my photography skills. Eventually, I would like to expand this portfolio to include my school work as well.

Hope you can find your way around!
